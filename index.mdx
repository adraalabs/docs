Adraa Inbox is an omnichannel customer conversation platform — web chat, WhatsApp, and email in one shared inbox. The Adraa Inbox API lets your own systems act on those conversations: route them to the right agent and reply to customers programmatically.

Typical uses:

- Assign conversations from your CRM based on account ownership
- Send order updates or notifications into an existing conversation
- Hand a conversation to the workspace AI agent, or take it back
