A contact is a person who has messaged your workspace. Adraa Inbox collects what's known about them — across every channel and conversation — into one profile.

## Contacts directory

The **Contacts** page lists everyone who has reached your workspace, folded into one row per person even when they've messaged on more than one channel. Filter the directory by:

- Country
- Channel
- Assignment (assigned, unassigned, or assigned to you)
- Whether they have an open conversation

## Contact panel

Open a conversation to see the contact panel on the right. It shows:

- **Identity** — name, avatar, country, and the channels they've used
- **Details** — email, phone, first contact date, and conversation counts
- **Metadata** — custom key-value fields (see below)
- **Flows** — automation flows that have run for this contact
- **Notes** — internal notes about the person, visible only to your team

## Custom metadata

Track anything you want about a contact with custom key-value fields. In the contact panel's **Metadata** section, enter a field name and value to add one, and remove a field with the delete button next to it.

Metadata is shared with fields collected automatically by [flows](/guide/flows) and imports, so a customer's full record stays in one place.

<Note>
  Custom fields are also returned by the
  [Contacts API](/api-reference/introduction), so you can read and update them
  from your own systems.
</Note>
