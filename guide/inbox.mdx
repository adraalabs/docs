The inbox is the shared workspace where your team handles customer conversations. Every channel feeds into the same list, and each conversation opens as a thread you can reply to, assign, and annotate.

## Conversations

Each conversation is one thread with a customer. The list shows the contact, a channel icon, the last message, and unread counts. Open a conversation to see the full message history and reply.

Conversations are either **open** or **closed**. Closing one removes it from the active list; if the customer messages again, it reopens automatically.

## Assignment

Assign a conversation to an agent so it's clear who owns it. You can assign to:

- A specific teammate
- An [agent group](/guide/team#agent-groups), to route it to a team
- The workspace AI agent, which replies automatically (see [AI replies](/guide/ai-replies))

Assignments update for everyone in real time.

## Replying

Reply from the composer at the bottom of the thread. Depending on the channel you can:

- Send text
- Attach images, video, audio, and files
- Reply to a specific earlier message (quoted reply)
- React to a message with an emoji

Replies are delivered on the conversation's channel and appear in the thread immediately.

## Internal notes

Leave internal notes on a conversation to share context with your team. Notes are visible only to your workspace — never to the customer — and show inline in the thread. See [Contacts](/guide/contacts) for notes that stay attached to a person across conversations.

## Real-time updates

New messages, assignments, and status changes appear instantly for everyone viewing the workspace, on web and in the iOS app, with push notifications for new messages.
