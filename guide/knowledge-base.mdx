The knowledge base is the content the workspace AI draws on when it answers customers. Add your documentation and website pages, and the AI grounds its [replies](/guide/ai-replies) in them instead of guessing.

Manage sources in the agent console under **Knowledge**.

## Sources

Add knowledge from:

- **Files** — upload documents your team maintains.
- **Website** — point the crawler at your site to import pages as content.

Adraa Inbox indexes each source so the AI can retrieve the most relevant passages for a given customer message.

## How it's used

When the AI generates a reply — either as an automatic answer or from a flow's AI response node — it retrieves matching content from the knowledge base and uses it to ground the response. Better and more complete sources lead to better answers.

<Note>
  Knowledge retrieval is only active when the workspace has the AI features
  configured. Without it, the AI replies from its instructions alone.
</Note>
