A workspace is shared by your team. Manage who has access, what they can do, and how conversations route between them under **Settings → Members**.

## Members and roles

Each member has one of two roles:

- **Admin** — full access, including managing members, channels, billing, and workspace settings.
- **Agent** — works conversations and contacts, without workspace administration.

Admins can change a member's role or remove them from the workspace.

<Note>
  You can't change your own role. Ask another admin if your role needs to
  change — this prevents a workspace from being left without an admin by
  accident.
</Note>

## Inviting teammates

Invite someone by email and pick the role they'll get when they join. Pending invites show in the members list until accepted, and you can revoke one at any time.

## Agent groups

An agent group is a named set of agents you can route conversations to as a unit, instead of picking one person. Groups are useful for:

- **Assignment** — hand a conversation to a whole team from the inbox or a [flow](/guide/flows).
- **Email routing** — send mail for a specific address (such as `billing@yourcompany.com`) to the right group. See [Email](/guide/email#routing-inbound-email).

Create and manage groups in workspace settings.
