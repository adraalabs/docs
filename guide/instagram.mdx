Connect an Instagram professional account so Instagram Direct messages arrive in your inbox and your team can reply from the conversation thread.

## Before you connect

Your Instagram account must be a professional (business or creator) account linked to a Facebook Page you manage. The connection uses Facebook Login and the Page you choose.

## Connect an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Instagram settings">
    In the agent console, go to **Settings → Channels → Instagram**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect account">
    Select **Connect account** and sign in with Facebook. Grant access to the
    Page and its linked Instagram account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm it's active">
    The account appears in the list as active once connected and subscribed to
    message updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Incoming DMs, story replies, and story mentions show up as conversations, identified by the customer's Instagram username.

## The reply window

Instagram lets you reply for free for 24 hours after the customer's last message. Replies sent between 24 hours and 7 days are delivered using the human-agent tag, which Adraa Inbox applies automatically.

After 7 days the window is closed and the customer has to message you again before you can reply.

<Note>
  If you try to reply after the window has closed, the send is rejected with a
  message explaining why. Nothing is lost — the draft stays in the composer.
</Note>

## What you can send

- Text replies
- Images, video, and audio
